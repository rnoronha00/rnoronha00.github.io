About Me

My name is Roshan Noronha and welcome to my site! I'm a bioinformation from Vancouver BC, Canada and passionate about Biology, genetics and computer science. I'll be posting the projects I am working on, interesting code that I am writing, papers that I am reading and other cool things.

I enjoy learning new software and languages and applying them to the projects that I work on. One of my current projects is an app that can find open reading frames in a string of DNA.
